Particle Properties

Sand: Falls downwards.

Water: Flows downwards and spreads.

Fire: Burns and creates smoke, can ignite other materials.

Oil: Floats on water and can cause explosions.

Acid: Corrodes metals and interacts with other materials.

Glass: Replaces sand when heated.

Smoke: Rises and spreads.

Gas: Rises and spreads quickly.

Steam: Produced by heating water.
